Lord, When Will I Get My Rose?

You don't want to miss this heartfelt gospel stage play that tells the story of 30 year old Latisha Johnson, a single mother who is a very educated, successful, self sufficient, Christian woman but tired of being alone. Latisha spends all of her time raising her two sons, working in upper management at a very prominent company and as the church clerk but she has come to a place in her life she is ready to settle down and be married but is tired of waiting on the Lord for the spouse he ordained for her. Since God isn't moving fast enough join us on January 12, 2019 7:00 PM – 10:00 PM EST at the Hollywood Central Performing Arts Center 1770 Monroe Street Hollywood, FL 33020 to see what happens when Latisha takes matters into her own hands and what the outcome is going to be.

The Author

Adeleane Dixon is the 9th child of the late Rev. John T and Mrs Mamie L. Haymon of Penney Farms, Fl. She has been married to her College sweetheart for 30 years Martin Dixon and they were blessed with four children Victoria, Christina, Martin Jr. and Marlin.They have six grandchildren whom she refers to as her "Six Heartbeats". She has been a passionate writer since the age of 15 and is the founder of the Morning Visions Productions Co in which she takes pride in introducing children to the performing arts. She is the writer, producer, director of several hit gospel stage plays.

Literary Works

Adeleane Dixon is the co-writer of 50 Shades of Change and author of What happened to my Rock? the story of the emotional journey she experienced while caring for her parents who had Alzheimer's and Dementia. The book will be in bookstores in the Spring of 2019.

Theatrical Productions

Adeleane is the writer, producer, director of the hit gospel stage play The a Time Is Now; the play the will preach, the black history stage play The Blood Crying From the Ground; A story of sacrifice from Slavery to Freedom and now the soon to be blockbuster hit play Lord, When Will I Get My Rose?
