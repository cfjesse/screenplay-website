Lord When Will I Get A Rose?

You don't want to miss this heartfelt gospel stage play that tells the story of 30 year old Latisha Johnson, a single mother who is a very educated, successful, self sufficient, Christian woman but tired of being alone. Latisha spends all of her time raising her two sons, working in upper management at a very prominent company and as the church clerk but she has come to a place in her life she is ready to settle down and be married but is tired of waiting on the Lord for the spouse he ordained for her. Since God isn't moving fast enough join us on January 12, 2019 to see what happens when Latisha takes matters into her own hands and what the outcome is going to be. Get Your Ticket Now!
